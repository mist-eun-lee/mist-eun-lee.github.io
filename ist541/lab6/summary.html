4 Steps to Reduce Stress at Work

Home See the signs Take breaks Manage priorities Set boundaries Summary

Recap

  1. Recognize the signs: Notice early signals - tension, fatigue, and distraction.
  2. Take breaks: Even 5 minutes restores clarity and lowers stress.
  3. Manage priorities: Do first, schedule next, delegate, then delete.
  4. Set boundaries: Power down, say no to after-hours pings, protect your time.
